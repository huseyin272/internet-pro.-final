Iztuzu Beach

About

Iztuzu Beach, also known as Turtle Beach, is a pristine stretch of coastline located near Dalyan in southwestern Turkey. Renowned for its natural beauty and conservation efforts, the beach attracts visitors seeking relaxation and environmental awareness.

Location

Iztuzu Beach is situated between the Mediterranean Sea and the Dalyan River delta. Accessible by boat from Dalyan or by road from the nearby town of Ortaca, the beach offers stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Activities

Visitors to Iztuzu Beach can enjoy a variety of activities, including sunbathing, swimming, and birdwatching. The beach is also a nesting ground for loggerhead sea turtles, providing opportunities for turtle watching and conservation awareness.

Visit

Iztuzu Beach is open to visitors from April to October, with facilities including sun loungers, umbrellas, and beachside cafes. Environmental regulations are in place to protect the nesting areas of sea turtles, ensuring a sustainable and enjoyable beach experience.
