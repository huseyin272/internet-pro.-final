Tlos Ancient City

About

Tlos is an ancient Lycian city located near the modern town of Fethiye in Turkey. It is one of the oldest and most important settlements in the Lycian region, with a history dating back over 4,000 years.

History

Tlos has a rich and diverse history, having been inhabited by various civilizations including the Lycians, Greeks, and Romans. It served as an important commercial and political center, with its strategic location along trade routes.

Architecture

The ancient city of Tlos features impressive architectural remains, including a well-preserved acropolis, theater, stadium, baths, and necropolis. The site offers insights into Lycian urban planning, engineering, and artistic traditions.

Visit

Today, visitors to Tlos can explore the ancient ruins and admire the picturesque mountain scenery surrounding the site. Guided tours are available to learn about Tlos's history, mythology, and archaeological significance.
