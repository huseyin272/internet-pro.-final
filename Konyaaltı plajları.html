Konyaaltı Beaches

About

Konyaaltı Beaches are a popular coastal destination located in the city of Antalya, Turkey. Stretching along the Mediterranean coastline, these beaches offer visitors a beautiful setting for sunbathing, swimming, and enjoying water sports.

Features

The beaches boast clear turquoise waters, soft sandy shores, and stunning views of the surrounding mountains. Facilities such as sunbeds, umbrellas, showers, and beach bars are available for visitors' convenience.

Activities

Visitors to Konyaaltı Beaches can enjoy a variety of activities, including swimming, snorkeling, jet skiing, and beach volleyball. The calm and shallow waters make it an ideal spot for families with children.

Visit

Konyaaltı Beaches are easily accessible from the city center of Antalya, with public transportation options available. The beaches are free to access and are open year-round, making them a popular destination for locals and tourists alike.
