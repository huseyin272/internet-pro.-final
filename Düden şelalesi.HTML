Düden Waterfalls

About

Düden Waterfalls are a series of stunning waterfalls located in the province of Antalya, Turkey. They are formed by the Düden River, which cascades over steep cliffs into the Mediterranean Sea.

Geography

The Düden Waterfalls are divided into two main sections: the Upper Düden Waterfalls (Yukarı Düden Şelalesi) and the Lower Düden Waterfalls (Aşağı Düden Şelalesi). The upper falls are situated inland, while the lower falls flow directly into the sea near the city center of Antalya.

Features

The Lower Düden Waterfalls are particularly striking, as they plunge over a rocky cliff directly into the Mediterranean Sea, creating a breathtaking spectacle. Visitors can admire the falls from various viewpoints and take boat tours to see them up close.

Visit

Düden Waterfalls are easily accessible from the city of Antalya, making them a popular tourist destination. Visitors can explore the surrounding park areas, enjoy picnics with scenic views, and take boat trips to experience the beauty of the falls from the sea.
