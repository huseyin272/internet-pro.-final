Hıdırnebi Yaylası

Location

Hıdırnebi Yaylası is a high-altitude plateau located in the Çaykara district of Trabzon province, Turkey.

Altitude

The plateau is situated at an elevation of approximately 1,600 meters (5,249 feet) above sea level.

Natural Beauty

Hıdırnebi Yaylası is renowned for its lush green meadows, panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, and tranquil atmosphere.

Activities

Popular activities in Hıdırnebi Yaylası include hiking, trekking, picnicking, and enjoying traditional Turkish cuisine at local restaurants.

Attractions

Key attractions near Hıdırnebi Yaylası include the Hıdırnebi Plateau Mosque, Hıdırnebi Waterfall, and nearby forests teeming with diverse wildlife.
