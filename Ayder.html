Ayder

About

Ayder is a picturesque mountain village located in the Çamlıhemşin district of Rize province, Turkey. Situated in the lush greenery of the Kaçkar Mountains, Ayder is renowned for its natural beauty, hot springs, and traditional wooden houses.

Attractions

One of the main attractions in Ayder is its thermal hot springs, which are believed to have therapeutic properties. Visitors can relax and unwind in the warm mineral-rich waters while enjoying panoramic views of the surrounding mountains.

Accommodation

Ayder offers a range of accommodation options, including cozy guesthouses, boutique hotels, and campsites. Many of these accommodations are traditional wooden houses, providing visitors with an authentic mountain village experience.

Visit

Ayder is accessible by road from the city of Rize, with scenic drives through the mountains. Visitors can explore the village on foot, hike along nearby trails, and immerse themselves in the tranquility of nature.
