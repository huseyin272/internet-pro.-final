Museum of Anatolian Civilizations

Location

The Museum of Anatolian Civilizations is located in Ankara, the capital city of Turkey. It is situated in the historic Atpazarı district, near Ankara Castle.

About

The museum showcases the rich cultural heritage of Anatolia, spanning over several millennia. It houses artifacts and exhibits from various civilizations that flourished in the region, including the Hittites, Phrygians, Urartians, and Romans.

Exhibits

The exhibits at the museum include pottery, sculptures, jewelry, tools, and other artifacts that provide insight into the daily life, religious practices, and artistic achievements of ancient Anatolian civilizations.

Visit

The Museum of Anatolian Civilizations is open to visitors throughout the week, except Mondays. Visitors can explore the museum's galleries, attend guided tours, and learn about the fascinating history of Anatolia.
