Antalya Aquarium

About

Antalya Aquarium is one of the largest aquarium complexes in the world, located in the city of Antalya, Turkey. It offers visitors an immersive journey into the underwater world, showcasing a wide variety of marine life from different ecosystems.

Exhibits

The aquarium features numerous exhibits, including large tanks with tropical fish, sharks, and other marine creatures. Visitors can explore themed zones representing different aquatic environments, such as coral reefs, tropical forests, and freshwater rivers.

Activities

In addition to viewing marine life, Antalya Aquarium offers various activities for visitors of all ages. These include interactive touch pools, underwater tunnels for close-up views of marine creatures, and educational programs about marine conservation.

Visit

Antalya Aquarium is open to visitors year-round, providing an enjoyable and educational experience for families, school groups, and tourists. Tickets can be purchased online or at the aquarium entrance, and guided tours are available for those who want to learn more about the exhibits.
