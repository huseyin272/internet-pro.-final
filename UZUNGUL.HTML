Uzungöl

Location

Uzungöl is a lake situated in the Çaykara district of Trabzon province, Turkey.

Size

The lake is approximately 1,100 meters (3,610 feet) long and 500 meters (1,640 feet) wide.

Surroundings

Uzungöl is surrounded by dense forests and steep cliffs, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains.

Activities

Popular activities in Uzungöl include sightseeing, boat tours on the lake, hiking, and sampling local cuisine at the nearby restaurants.

Attractions

Key attractions near Uzungöl include the Uzungöl Mosque, Uzungöl Nature Park, and the nearby waterfalls.
