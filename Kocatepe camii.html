Kocatepe Mosque

About

The Kocatepe Mosque is a prominent landmark and one of the largest mosques in Ankara, Turkey. It is situated in the Kocatepe neighborhood, near the city center.

Architecture

The mosque features an impressive Ottoman-inspired architectural design, characterized by its large central dome, minarets, and intricate decorative elements. The exterior is adorned with marble and ornate calligraphy.

Interior

The interior of the Kocatepe Mosque is spacious and elegantly decorated, with intricate patterns, colorful tiles, and ornamental details. The main prayer hall can accommodate thousands of worshippers.

Visit

The Kocatepe Mosque is open to visitors outside of prayer times. Visitors can admire its architecture, explore the surrounding area, and experience the spiritual atmosphere of this iconic mosque.
