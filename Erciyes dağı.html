Erciyes Dağı

Location

Erciyes Dağı, also known as Mount Erciyes, is a massive stratovolcano located in Kayseri province, central Turkey.

Geology

Mount Erciyes is an extinct volcano with an elevation of approximately 3,917 meters (12,848 feet) above sea level, making it the highest mountain in Central Anatolia.

Activities

Popular activities on Erciyes Dağı include skiing, snowboarding, mountaineering, hiking, and paragliding, attracting outdoor enthusiasts from around the world.

Attractions

Key attractions on Mount Erciyes include ski resorts, alpine meadows, and panoramic viewpoints offering stunning views of the surrounding landscapes.
