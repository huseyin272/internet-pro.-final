Yahyalı

Location

Yahyalı is a district in Kayseri province, central Turkey. It is situated in the historic region of Cappadocia.

History

Yahyalı has a rich history dating back to ancient times. It was inhabited by various civilizations, including the Hittites, Persians, Romans, and Byzantines.

Culture

The district is known for its unique cultural heritage, traditional crafts, and vibrant festivals celebrating local customs and traditions.

Attractions

Key attractions in Yahyalı include historical landmarks, traditional villages, natural landscapes, and opportunities for outdoor activities such as hiking and exploring.
